## Plot Overview:

The movie **_Sir_** is about a teacher named **Gnanam** who comes back to his village to help kids learn even though they don't have any facility to learn or study. This village is called Maangkollai, and it's a small place where not many people know how to read or write or even go to school to get education. Gnanam's dad, Arasan, started a school there, and now Gnanam wants to take care of it. At first, Gnanam doesn't agree with how his dad runs things, but later he understands and works with him.

In the village, some people don't like the idea of kids going to school. **Sakthivel** is the bad guy who wants to stop the school because he thinks the land on which the school was built belongs to him. Gnanam and his dad have to fight against many problems, like mean villagers, unfair rules, and people spreading lies, fighting with the goons not only this but many, to keep the school running.

The story shows how learning can make life better for everyone. **Gnanam** wants kids in the village to grow up smart and strong, but it's hard because the bad guy and his friends try to stop them.
