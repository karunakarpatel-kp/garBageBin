This movie is about a real-life hero named **Mukund Varadharajan** where He was an brave and young dynamic soldier who loved his family and his country as if they were his both eyes. The story was told by his wife, Indhu, who is going to get a big award Ashok Chakra on behalf of him at the Indian President Office because of **Mukund** achievements in the Army.

**Indhu** and **Mukund** met in college. Mukund helped her with a fashion show, and they became good friends. Soon, both of them were fell in love. Mukund wanted to join the Army to protect people, but his mom didn't like that idea because she was scared for him. But Mukund didn't give up and passed the Army test.

At first, their families didn't like each other, but later they agreed, and Mukund and Indhu got married. They were very happy and had a baby girl named **Arsheya**. Mukund worked very hard and became a Captain in the Army.
One day, Mukund was sent to Kashmir, a place where bad guys were causing trouble. Mukund and his team had to find and stop them. Mukund was very brave and worked with his team to protect people. They fought against a bad guy named Altaf Baba and won the fight!

But soon, another bad guy named Wani started making trouble. Mukund and his team had to stop him too. Even when Mukund got hurt, he didn't stop. He told Indhu, **“Promise me you won't cry, even if something happens to me.”**

Mukund and his team found out where Wani was hiding. It was very dangerous, but Mukund and his teammate Vikram went inside to catch him. They fought bravely, but **Mukund** got hurt very badly.
